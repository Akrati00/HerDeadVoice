Her Dead Voice..

Shattered Innocence,Echoing Change..

This Book is dedicated to the girls and women whose voices have been silenced by sexual abuse and oppression...May this book serve as a testament to your strength, your resilience, and your unwavering spirit. To the society and the loved ones of those girls she is suffering,she needs to be heard,let her screams shout so loud to tremble the monster's soul for such an act he did. Let her be the voice of her, her sisters who suffered the same way in this world. Some still live with hearts full of injuries and some took relief from it by dying with such intense pain.We see you, we hear you, and we stand with you in solidarity.May your stories ignite a flame of hope and spark a revolution of change..

The Author

Abhinav Patel

Abhinav is someone who loves to deep dive into everything that he loves . He loves to explore things at his best . He loves to learn,he loves to invent,sometimes he thinks that he can change the world .He can stop cruelty to every innocent being but maybe that's only his dream.That can't be true. He loves to give message of kindness , mercy,compassion,and justice . That's why he himself is a vegan. He tries his best to reduce suffering that's why he chose to become vegan. He is always on the lookout for new ideas and ways to improve the world around him. He is so empathetic that he cannot bring himself to harm even a mosquito, because he believes every innocent being has a right to live freely and happily .

The Author

Aditi Jogdand

Aditi is the one who does what she likes and feels to do. She is still unknown to her , in their journey of finding herself. She wants to change this world to the best, most beautiful world for each being to live in. Just don't know if it is possible or not. She is blessed with sweet, caring parents, who still unlearn and learn things to make her life better,beautiful than before. Her elder brother is the only person who taught her the real meaning of kindness to all.He is the reason she became a kind vegan like him. Not like him because there's no one in this world like her brother and never will be. She is blessed to have a brother like him. And an elder sister who's lifetime strength and happiness, a fairy who brings infinite colours in her life.
